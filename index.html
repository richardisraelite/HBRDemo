Maritime 21' Power Boats
Our 21' Maritime center console boats accommodate up to 4–6 people comfortably. They offer a soft dry ride powered by quiet and efficient Yamaha 4-stroke outboard motors. Outfitted with the serious fisherman in mind, they are also great for coastal cruising and whale watching.

Fuel: Boats are fueled and ready to go. Renters must refuel at the Dana Point Fuel Dock before returning.
Bait: Live bait at the Dana Point bait barge. Frozen bait at the Dana Point Fuel Dock.
Cleaning: Rinse with fresh water and clean trash, fish blood, guts, scales & slime upon return. Hose, bucket, soap, brush & towels provided.
